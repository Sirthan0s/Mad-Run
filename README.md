## The Story

In a world where every blockchain promise could be a rug pull, every Discord message could be FUD, and every new token could be a scam — one mad scientist decided he had enough.

Deep inside his lab on the Cosmos Hub, Dr. ATOM spent years developing the ultimate formula: a way to filter signal from noise, truth from manipulation, and real value from empty hype. But the lab was compromised. The FUD Bot broke through the firewall. The Rug Lord pulled the floor from under him. The Scam King locked the exit.

Now he has one chance to escape.

Armed with nothing but his flask, his lab coat, and a handful of bullets — he runs.

Every ATOM orb he collects fuels his journey. Every RUG he dodges is a protocol that didn't get him. Every FUD bubble he destroys is a lie that didn't stick.

Three bosses stand between him and freedom. Defeat them all and escape the lab.

**The Run has begun. Will you make it out?**

## ⚡ How It Works

### Hazards falling from above
| Object | Effect |
|---|---|
| 🟥 **RUG PULL** | −1 life on contact · shoot to destroy |
| 💬 **FUD Bubble** | −1 life on contact · shoot to destroy |
| ⚛ **ATOM Orb** | +0.10 ATOM + random power-up bonus |
| 🟩 **AMMO Pack** | +4 bullets |
| ❤️ **Heart** | +1 life (drops during boss fights) |

### ATOM Orb Bonuses (random each time)
| Bonus | Effect |
|---|---|
| 🔫 Ammo +4 | Reload bullets |
| ❤️ Life +1 | Extra life |
| 🛡️ Shield 3s | Full invincibility |
| ⚡ Rapid Fire 5s | Machine gun mode |
| 🐢 Slow 4s | All hazards slow down |
| 💣 Clear Screen | Destroys everything instantly |

### Boss Fights — Levels 3, 6 & 10
| Level | Boss | HP | Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3 | 🤖 **FUD BOT** | 12 HP | 3-way aimed spread |
| 6 | 🎰 **RUG LORD** | 16 HP | 4-way radial + rug drops |
| 10 | 👑 **SCAM KING** | 20 HP | 3-burst laser burst |

- Bosses **speed up and shoot faster** at low HP
- AMMO packs and Hearts drop during boss fights
- Defeat a boss: **+1/+2/+3 lives** + **+5 ammo**
- **Defeat the SCAM KING at level 10 → you escape the lab → game won**

### Ammo System
Start with 5 bullets. Ammo is limited — shoot hazards and bosses, collect AMMO packs to reload. Run out and you can't shoot until you find more. Each level-up grants +3 ammo +1 life.

### Leaderboard
Set your nickname before playing. Your score (level reached, survival time, ATOM collected) saves automatically when the run ends and ranks against every other player globally.

## ⛓️ Cosmos Hub Connection

- **Keplr Wallet** — connect via `cosmoshub-4`
- **ATOM Rewards** — every orb = +0.10 ATOM tracked live in the sidebar
- **Bonding Curve** — ticket price rises as more players join
- **Gacha Traits** — AI-analyzed power-up traits unlocked after each run
- **Global Leaderboard** — real-time ranked competition between all players
- **Share on X** — post your score and ATOM earned

The game is a risk/reward simulation: players manage limited ammo, trade survival for ATOM collection, and make split-second decisions under pressure — mirroring real onchain incentive dynamics.

## 🤖 AI Usage

Built with Claude as an active co-builder and embedded in-game:

- **In-game AI mechanic** — after every run, Claude analyzes your play style (lives lost, ATOM collected, ammo efficiency, win/loss) via the Anthropic API and generates 2 personalized Gacha Traits with reasoning, instead of static random rewards
- Game engine architecture, canvas rendering, and boss AI pattern design
- Incentive mechanics design (ammo economy, ATOM bonuses, gacha system)
- Pixel mascot sprite integration and asset processing
- Iterative playtesting and bug fixing through conversation
